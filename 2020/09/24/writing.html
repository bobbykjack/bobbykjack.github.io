Switch Player & Ninty Fresh magazines

In February 2020, I began writing reviews and contributing features for Switch Player Magazine, an online and print publication. As of July 2022, I have written 42 reviews.

The highlight was undoubtedly my review for Super Mario 3D All-Stars which was afforded a five-page spread in the October 2020 issue. I also wrote a feature retrospective of Baba Is You for the July 2020 magazine.

My role has since developed with promotions to Reviews Editor and, in 2021, Editor-in-Chief. As Editor, I take overall responsibility for the magazine, develop new features, proof & edit each issue, and manage the team. I continue to write content and manage the review process in this role.

Make Use Of

In October 2020, I started a position as Staff Writer at MakeUseOf. The role demands pitching ideas for articles relating to technology in the wider sense; I currently specialise in topics covering programming, gaming, and the explanation of technical concepts.

In July 2021 I took on a role as a Floating Editor which involves proofing and editing articles on a daily basis. In March 2022, I started working as an Editor for the Programming section of the site.
